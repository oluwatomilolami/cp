About Truthshare

Getting Counselling can be a bit on the hard side. When you need someone to talk to about various crisis in your life, from depression to loneliness to things not working. You find that you either have to go to a church or your parents or friends, when you would have preferred to do it quietly or meet a counsellor.

Truthshare is here to help ease depression and improve the mental health of Africans. Feel better when you write anonymously & get access to certified & qualified counsellors
